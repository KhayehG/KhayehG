### Hey, I'm Khayelihle.

I'm a **full-stack developer** based in **South Africa**, and I graduated from **Durban University of Technology** with a Diploma in **ICT Application Development**.

I care about shipping software that solves real problems: solid backends, clear APIs, and interfaces people can actually use. I'm especially comfortable in the **ASP.NET / C#** world and modern **React** stacks, and I'm always stretching into **mobile (Flutter)** and **AI-assisted** workflows where they fit the product.

- **Exploring:** Mobile apps, AI-powered systems  
- **Mindset:** Always learning, always building
